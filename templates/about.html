Make an authentication system on your own where:

 

  • Add a bootstrap navbar where there will be Sign Up and Login for unauthenticated users and Profile and Logout for authenticated users.
  • A user can create an account.
  • Can log in to his account and after login redirect the user to his/her profile and show the message “Logged In Successfully”.
  • Can log out and after logout showed the message on the home page “Logged Out Successfully”.
  • An authenticated user can only change his password with and without a previous password.
